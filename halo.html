Halo Lore

Characters:

The Master Chief

The main protagonist of the Halo series. He is usually accompanied by his A.I. partner Cortana (more on her soon!), and more recently, Blue team.

The Master Chief is a spartan of the UNSC. The spartan II program was the program that chief was a part of and it made him into the soldier he is today.

At the age of 6, John was kidnapped and was put into the program to make a type of super soldier out of him and the other candidates. He and blue team are the only remaining spartan II's left alive.

Cortana

The A.I. partner of the Master Chief who is with him for the majority of the games exept for Halo 3 in which she is with him only in the last part of the second final mission and the final mission. Cortana is in Halo 5 but not with Chief she is actually in a Forerunner realm called the Domain.
